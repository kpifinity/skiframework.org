Why Continuum

The problem with living inside someone else's chat window

Your most useful thinking is scattered across Claude, ChatGPT and Grok — and locked in apps you don't control. Hit a usage limit and the thread stalls. Continuum fixes that without sending a single message to anyone.

Everything in one place

Import your conversations from Claude, ChatGPT and Grok and search across all of them at once — finally, one memory instead of three silos.

Never stop at a limit

When you hit a usage cap, keep the conversation going with a free local AI on your own machine — then hand it back to your favourite app with the context intact.

Truly private

Continuum is local-first. Your chats are stored only on your computer. The app's one outbound connection is an update check — never your conversations.

Yours to verify

Every saved thread is cryptographically signed and recorded in a tamper-evident ledger, so you can prove your archive is intact and unaltered.

How it works

From scattered chats to one archive you control

Bring your chats in

Paste a conversation, or drop in the data export from Claude or ChatGPT. Continuum reads them all.

Search & revisit

One search box across every app. Ask a question and get an answer drawn from your own past conversations.

Keep going, free

Hit a limit? Continue the thread with a free local model on your computer — replies stream in as they're written.

Hand back to your AI

Copy a clean, ready-to-paste summary to resume in Claude, ChatGPT or Grok right where you left off.

Sovereign by design

Privacy isn't a setting. It's the architecture.

Continuum is built on SKI — Sovereign Knowledge Intelligence — the same open framework KpiFinity built for regulated industries. Your data stays yours, provably.

  • Your conversations live only on your computer, in a folder you own.
  • The local AI runs on your machine — no prompts or replies sent to a cloud.
  • Signed, hash-chained history you can independently verify.
  • Anonymous usage counting is strictly opt-in, and off by default.
Local storage — ~/.continuum on your disk
Offline AI — runs on your CPU/GPU via Ollama
Signed ledger — every thread verifiable
Outbound — update check only, never your chats
